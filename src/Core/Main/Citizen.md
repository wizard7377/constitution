### 
::: Comment 
Residency:
Affirmed by the supreme court in many cases, but codified here 
:::

*Anyone, who may legally reside in one state, or a territory, shall be able to reside in any other state, or a territory.*
*No state may prohibit the movement of people into or out of that state.*

### 
::: Comment 
Birthright citizenship 
Included in the 14th amendment, however, emphasized in light of recent events
:::

Any person born in the United States, or territories thereof, shall be considered a US citizen, *no matter that person's lineage*.
Any person born to US citizens is a US citizen.

### 

::: Comment
Naturalization and Asylum 
:::

The right of non-citizens who reasonably fear for their safety to seek asylum in the United States shall not be infringed.
No person shall be arbitrary denied naturalization, nor shall the process be unduly difficult, nor shall they be deprived of their lawful residence without due process of law. 

### 

::: Comment
Protection of Citizenship 
:::

Citizenship may not be taken away from a US citizen for any purpose, including high treason.
No personal shall be intimidated, coerced, forced, ordered, sentenced, or in any other way pushed to give up their United States citizenship.
Further, no person shall be promised reward or in any other way incentivized to give up their citizenship.
