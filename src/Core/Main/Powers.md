
##

###

All powers ***actually and directly*** necessary and proper to carry into effect the forthcoming powers shall be considered part of those powers.
Any powers given to congress may be delegated to the states or executive, but, upon a simple majority in either house, such powers shall return to Congress.
All the following powers shall exist only insofar long as they do not come against the basic opposition of the people's rights.

###

The Federal Government, and not the states, shall have the power to:


- to regulate the transport of goods across state lines for the express purpose of selling them
- to regulate trade and commerce with other nations
- to create immigration law  
- *to establish regulations for banking, and create a bank of the United States*
- *to establish laws governing intellectual property*
- to establish federal courts beneath the Supreme Court
- to declare war, with a 3/5 majority of the house or 2/3 of the senate 
- to establish rules of the military  
- to provide for the army  
- to define punishments for crimes against the United States
- To impeach officials, with a 2/3 majority

### 

Both the Federal Goverment, and the states shall have the power to:

- to borrow money  
- *to provide for the public good*
- *to ensure a well-educated citizenry*
- *to create public works, places, and parks*
- to promote the development of academia, and of art  
- *to regulate, prohibit, or otherwise restrict the trade of all firearms* <!-- Second amendment goes -->

###

The states, and not the Federal government, shall have the power to:

- **To create any law governing morality, unless such is deemed necessary the civility of a society**
- create any other law, as well as to create regulations for such laws


### 

Neither the Federal government nor the states shall have the power to:
- create any law that establishes, forbids, or has the sole effect of encouraging or discouraging the follows of a certain religion, or religion in general.
- restrict the movement of people across state lines, and within the states
